Behind Our Produce

Himalayan Sourcing & Sourcing

We work with local mountain cooperatives at high altitudes to source authentic ingredients. Click the options below to explore our sourcing stories.

Traditional Ghatt Mills

Our local millets and grains are ground using traditional stone watermills (Ghatts) powered entirely by glacial streams. This slow, cold-grinding process retains all natural nutrients and delivers a superior texture to the flour.

💧

Eco-Friendly Hydropower

Zero electricity, utilizing natural glacial stream currents.
