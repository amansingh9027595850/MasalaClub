The Spirit of Chamoli

Our Story & Philosophy

High up in the Chamoli district, surrounded by towering peaks and clean mountain air, we envisioned Masala Club. It isn't just a place to eat—it is a community hub that connects travelers and locals through honest, warm food and sustainably harvested valley produce.

We work closely with local self-help groups and organic farmers across Uttarakhand, sourcing aromatic cardamoms, wild mountain honey, and hand-rolled tea leaves directly, ensuring fair prices for the hills.

Specials Sourced at 1,550m

Wild Rhododendron Buransh

Harvested during spring from the crimson forest canopies surrounding Chamoli, the Buransh flower is renowned for its heart-healthy nutrients and crisp cooling flavor. We hand-press the petals to create our seasonal Rhododendron drinks and jarred syrups.

Try it fresh at our cafe counter or pick up a bottle from our grocery catalog to savor the high Himalayas from home.
